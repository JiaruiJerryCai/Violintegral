Words from the Founder

This website is where I post organized notes I made for my students that I tutor (high school and college undergraduate students) and the general public. The math and physics resources are 100% free. Viewers are not permitted to privately distribute the resources (Exception: practice problems) as these resources are subject to copyright. Anyone who wants to access and view these resources must access them through this website. Sorry for the inconvenience and thank you for your understanding!


Viewers, feel free to share this website with people you know!
Teachers, feel free to use these resources as a supplement for your class by sharing this website with your students, which hopefully should be able to help them absorb the material better!


Thank you for your trust in me!
